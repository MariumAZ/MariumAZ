Book Reviews

Here are some books that have influenced my work:

Designing Machine Learning Systems: An Iterative Process for Production-Ready Applications

Author: Chip Huyen

This book provides a high level comprehensive guide to designing production-ready machine learning systems and offers practical advice for building scalable and efficient ML solutions.

Lean In: Women, Work and the Will to Lead

Author: Sheryl Sandberg

Sheryl Sandberg's "Lean In" encourages women to pursue their ambitions and challenge the barriers that hold them back, providing insights into leadership and gender equality in the workplace. Though, I feel like the book takes a position towards a specific religion which made it biased unfortunately.
